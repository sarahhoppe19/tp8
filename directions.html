• In a large skillet, cook beef and sausage over medium heat 6-8 minutes or until no longer pink, breaking into crumbles.
  • Drain all the meat and transfer to a 5- or 6-quart slow cooker.
  • Stir in beans, salsa, tomatoes, onion, tomato sauce, mushrooms, peppers and seasonings.
  • Cook, covered, on low 8-10 hours or until flavors are blended.
  • Add toppings of your choice and serve!
